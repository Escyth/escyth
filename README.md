## 🚀 What I'm Building

### [Raqib (راقب)](https://raqib.uaab.sa)
A monitoring platform for Saudi license plate auctions. Track Absher plate auctions in real time, get alerts, and never miss a bid.

### [Thaheen (ذهين)](https://thaheen.study)
A practice platform made for KFUPM students - sharpen your skills with high-quality problems and aim for the best scores.
- 1,000+ Questions
- 600+ Users
- 10+ Courses

### Track (Senior Capstone Project)
A Smart Warehouse Platform bringing AI and IoT tracking within reach of SMEs, powered by a triple AI architecture:

- **Sense AI:** Real-time inventory tracking via computer vision, load cells, and ESP32 microcontrollers. One rail, one moving camera, unlimited racks.
- **Think AI:** ML-driven demand forecasting with event-aware feature engineering (Ramadan, Eid, salary week, and other local demand patterns).
- **Speak AI:** An intelligent assistant turning raw data into dashboards, alerts, and actionable recommendations.

**Results:** 90%+ forecasting accuracy · sub-second device communication · ~12% reduction in inventory holding costs · full inventory update cycle in under 20 seconds.
